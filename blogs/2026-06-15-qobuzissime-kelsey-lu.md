Seven years after her 2019 debut album "Blood," singer and cellist Kelsey Lu returns with a cosmic sophomore project, "So Help Me God." The North Carolina-born art-pop innovator, rooted in blues and gospel, is profiled as an artist whose voice and movement are inseparable from her expression.
